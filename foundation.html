About the Foundation

The PhD.Hub Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing doctoral education and research globally. Established with the vision of breaking down barriers in academic research, we provide financial support, resources, and opportunities to PhD candidates and early-career researchers worldwide.

Our foundation believes that groundbreaking research should not be limited by financial constraints or lack of access to resources. We work tirelessly to level the playing field, ensuring that talented researchers from all backgrounds have the opportunity to pursue their academic dreams.

What We Do

Our programs and initiatives support researchers at every stage of their doctoral journey

Research Grants

Providing financial support for promising research projects across all disciplines

Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships for exceptional PhD candidates from underrepresented communities

Conference Travel

Travel grants to help researchers present their work at international conferences

Research Tools

Access to premium software, databases, and computational resources

Workshops & Training

Professional development programs on research methodology and academic writing

Research Awards

Annual awards recognizing outstanding doctoral research and innovation
