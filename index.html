Our Mission

PhD.Hub academic community was founded by Dr. Tran Quoc Thien with support and mentorship from Vietnamese professors living and teaching in developed countries worldwide. With over 110,000 members in and outside Vietnam, we've built a centralized platform where members can search for and share information about full scholarships, academic experiences, scientific research, and support each other on the path to bringing Vietnamese knowledge to the international stage.

Hỗ trợ đóng phí visa du học với quỹ ban đầu là 50 triệu đồng cho 15-20 sinh viên mỗi năm. Nguồn quỹ sẽ kỳ vọng tăng lên 300 triệu đồng trong 3 năm.

FREE Mentorship

Mentor - Mentee matching up
